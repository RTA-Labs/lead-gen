# Investment Thesis

Private capital and alternative asset management firms that would benefit from AI readiness assessments and AI agent implementation — prospective clients for RTA Labs services.

## Target Firms

- **Private equity firms** — mid-market and lower-middle-market especially, where operational teams are lean and deal flow is manually managed
- **Venture capital firms** — firms managing portfolio operations, reporting, and deal pipelines across many companies
- **Family offices** — single and multi-family offices with active investment operations and limited technology staff
- **Fund administrators and fund services platforms** — back-office, NAV calculation, investor reporting, compliance workflows
- **Private credit / private debt firms** — underwriting, loan monitoring, covenant tracking with manual processes
- **Real estate PE / investment managers** — asset management, property-level data aggregation, investor reporting
- **Wealth management firms** — serving institutional or UHNW clients, especially those with complex reporting needs
- **Alternative asset managers** — infrastructure, natural resources, secondaries, or other alts with operational complexity

## Signals of AI Readiness Need

These indicate a firm is likely to benefit from AI services:

- Manual deal sourcing, due diligence, or portfolio monitoring processes
- Legacy technology stacks or spreadsheet-heavy workflows
- Growing AUM with flat headcount (operational scaling pressure)
- Recent fundraises (capital available to invest in operational improvements)
- Multiple portfolio companies or fund vehicles creating reporting complexity
- Regulatory or compliance burden handled through manual review

## Exclude

- **Quantitative hedge funds** — already have sophisticated technology teams and data infrastructure
- **Bulge-bracket banks and large asset managers** — have internal AI/ML teams and enterprise IT budgets
- **Crypto / DeFi funds** — different market, natively digital
- **Retail brokerages and B2C fintech** — consumer-facing, not our target client
- **Pure SaaS companies** — technology companies, not private capital firms
- **Consulting firms / IT services companies** — competitors, not clients
- **Government agencies and non-profits** — outside target market

The firm must operate in private capital, alternative assets, or institutional investment management. If the primary business is consumer financial services, technology products, or professional services, exclude it.
